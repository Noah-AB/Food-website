Directions

The Directions are

  • Step 1: Bring 2 1/2 cups of water to a boil in a medium saucepan
  • Step 2: Add Noodles, sriracha, kimchi, a drizzle of toasted sesame oil, furikake, and a wedge of lime (You can cut the lime in half or fourths, but everything is just added in the saucepan)
  • Step 3: Stir til it's all blended in the sause (except lime and noodles)
  • Step 4: Remove from heat
  • Step 5: Add the rest of the ingredients while stiring slightly so they mix together (make sure everything is cut down to small chunks before adding)
  • Step 6: Enjoy
